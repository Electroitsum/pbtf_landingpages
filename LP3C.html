HELPING CHILDREN THRIVE

www.curethekids.org/get-involved

PBTF offers a thriving community for children, survivors, and families. Together, we walk beside them from diagnosis through treatment and beyond.

Thanks to your gifts, we’re able to do this by:

  • Providing patient families with opportunities to connect with other families who have experiences like theirs.
  • Offering emotional and financial support to bereaved families following their child’s passing, wrapping our arms around them in support so they know they are not alone.
  • Providing resources and programs to help survivors and caregivers navigate the transition to adulthood and the lasting impact that diagnosis and treatment can have on a child’s quality of life.
  • Funding research to better understand the impact of chemotherapy and other “gold standard” treatments on children’s cognitive, behavioral, and physical development, as well as research into targeted treatments that could offer a true cure for this disease.

Join us in fighting for the future every child deserves.
